# Installation

![Onions5](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/98862735/179318051-39abe99e-79eb-43da-9778-aa9b4f6d1b28.png)


## <sup><img align="left" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/189428439-d70d00ac-3b1b-416f-a8fb-b47c8c6cead2.png" width="54" /></sup> Check your firmware

*The firmware is the backend part of the OS, which lives inside your device. Onion on the other hand is a frontend that will stay fresh on your SD card.*

You can check your current version in `Settings` › `Device Info` › `Version`.

<Tabs groupId="miyoo-version" queryString>
<TabItem value="mm" label="Miyoo Mini">


The April 2022 firmware from Miyoo (version `20220419****`) is the minimum requirement for Onion v3.10 and above.
Most devices produced after July 2022 will already have this firmware version.

:::caution
A bad firmware was found on devices bought in late December (version `20221216****` and `20221224****`).
Please apply the community patch found below.
:::

### Downloads

1. [Firmware 20220409](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1VtfcBCoIcpMIBY2FIyAtjV-Dg02JUvhG)
2. [Community patch (use only on firmware 20221216/24)](https://www.reddit.com/r/MiyooMini/comments/104qbak/community_firmware_patch_for_new_devices/)
